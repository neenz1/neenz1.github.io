Online Book of Dracula

This is a contemporary design play on the classic novel Dracula. I added contrast between the red & white to show hierarchy. Overall I wanted to tie the whole theme together by using a groovy font to reflect the era of Dracula. Fall ’19

Weather Page

A simplistic take on an online weather page. I wanted to be heavily icon based. I am from the Midwest so I included what I thought would be important information depending on the day. Fall ’19

Online Poems

Object was to design a poem stylized three different ways on three different screen sizes. Although the designs are different I wanted to play on the same through out by having a common theme, which is repetition.
